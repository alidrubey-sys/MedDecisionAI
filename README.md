
# MedDecisionAI

MedDecisionAI is an open-source AI-assisted clinical decision support project designed to help physicians structure medical reasoning, evaluate differential diagnoses, and support evidence-informed clinical and surgical decision workflows.

## Purpose

The goal of MedDecisionAI is not to replace physicians, but to support clinical reasoning by providing a structured second layer of analysis for medical decision-making.

This project aims to help with:

- Structured clinical reasoning
- Differential diagnosis support
- Surgical decision workflows
- Guideline-based thinking
- Risk-aware medical decision support
- Physician-oriented AI tools

## Why This Project Matters

Clinical decisions often require rapid synthesis of symptoms, laboratory findings, imaging results, comorbidities, guidelines, and surgical judgment.

MedDecisionAI is being developed from the perspective of a physician and general surgeon, with direct insight into real clinical workflows, hospital decision-making, and the practical needs of doctors.

## Target Users

- Physicians
- Surgeons
- Medical students
- Universities
- Hospitals
- Clinical AI researchers
- Healthcare software developers

## Current Status

This project is in an early development and research phase.

Planned focus areas include:

- Clinical decision support modules
- Differential diagnosis workflows
- Surgical risk assessment logic
- Guideline-based recommendation structures
- AI-assisted medical documentation
- Physician-facing interface design

## Important Medical Disclaimer

MedDecisionAI is intended for research, education, and physician-support purposes only.

It does not provide independent medical diagnosis or treatment decisions. All outputs must be reviewed and validated by qualified healthcare professionals. The final clinical decision always belongs to the responsible physician.
